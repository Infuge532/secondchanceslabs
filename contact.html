Common Questions

Before You Commission

How far out are you booked?

Capacity is limited — the shop runs a couple of overlapping projects at a time. Share your ideal timeline in the form and we'll tell you honestly where you'd land in the queue.

Do you take payment online?

We keep it simple: Venmo or cash. No credit cards, no processing fees hidden in the price of your piece.

Can you match my room's colors?

Yes. Between Eye Candy's pigment range and Nancy's interior-design eye, we'll blend a river color that belongs in your space — greens, blues, metallics, or crystal clear.

Do you deliver?

Local delivery and placement in the Kansas City area, and we'll arrange freight for pieces traveling farther. Conference tables are built in sections when needed.
